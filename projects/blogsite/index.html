Why I Choose Minimalism in My Design Work

When designing websites, it's tempting to add lots of effects and animations. For this project, I chose a minimalist approach instead. A simple design helps visitors focus on content without distractions. It also improves readability, loads faster, and is generally easier to maintain. By using clean typography, consistent spacing, and a limited colour palette, I was able to create a website that feels calm and easy to navigate.
